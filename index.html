Energy demand in India both in urban and rural areas in continuously increasing, however the electricity utilities are unable to meet this rapidly increasing demand. This is the reason why most of the commercial buildings such as hospitals, offices, malls etc. as well as residential buildings in the country are opting for back-up power systems. These back-up power systems are usually diesel generators. And with the present scenario of Shooting up electricity tariff rates and diesel prices, the maintenance costs for all domestic and commercial consumers, are also shooting up. The best alternative to reduce these electricity and diesel bills is solar rooftop systems. With recent down trend of solar system costs, solar rooftop systems have become a viable and attractive solution for all domestic and particularly commercial utilities.

With the aim of encouraging green initiative and to offer an economical and quality solution, Blu Energy offers rooftop solutions for all domestic and commercial utilities. With Blu Energy rooftop solution commercial utilities apart from saving their electricity, they can reduce their carbon footprint by reducing their diesel consumption and can claim accelerated depreciation for income tax benefits.

Blu Energy offers reliable and economical Off-Grid solar backup solutions for remote area applications where reliable grid supply will not be there or completely isolated from grid connection. As of now many off-grid applications like telecom towers, remote area banks are depending on diesel generator and battery backup for their day to day operations. With Blu energy Off-Grid solar solutions, all the off-grid remote applications can completely switch to solar power and apart from enjoying the reliable power supply, they can save considerably on consumption on diesel.

Blu Energy Off – Grid Solar Applications Include

  • Telecom Towers
  • Remote Bank branches and ATMs
  • Schools in Villages and Remote locations
  • Military Applications
  • Rural Electrification

As per the current scenario, even though government is offering free power for agriculture purpose, this free power is being supplied irregularly and at discrete intervals of time instead supplying for continuously. Because of this reason farmers being not able to supply enough water to the fields on correct times, resulting in reduced yields. And sometimes as the power is being supplied during night times, it becomes too difficult for the farmers to operate during night times.

To overcome these problems solar water pump is the best solution, as solar power is available throughout the day time farmers can run throughout day time and can eliminate night time operations. Blu Energy offers very economical and reliable water pump solution. With Blu Energy water pump solution any existing (submersible, ground mount, etc.) motor can be upgraded to solar water pump. Blu Energy water pump solution can operate only on solar power, without grid connection in case of remote locations.
